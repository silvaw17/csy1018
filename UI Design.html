Choices & Teamwork

I and my team spent a couple of hours on researching on one of the best user interfaces there are available on the internet, we came across so many that were just perfect.After an intensive research, we made up our mind up on how we were going to design our website. We stared the design for mobile first as more than 60% of users use mobile over a desktop computer. We had loads of issue on the way but we manage to fix it and get past it.We went with the company's logo colors which are dark red with white. we kept this way so that there aren't too many colors involved and making it unreadable. and kept on website simple at the same easy to use and up to date layout. We did the same layout for our website pages so that the website looks official and tidy. as we have couple people designing the website it would look the same instead of everyone doing something different. someone from our team done the base of the site and everyone else done the other pages design and content on it. Like this, the site will get done quicker .
